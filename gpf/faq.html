Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I would like to submit a paper to this special issue, but I am not sure if my paper would be considered a GPF. How will you determine that a paper satisfies the GPF requirements?

A: The general criteria are specified in the call for papers. In addition, the training materials include a checklist for authors. We also recommend participating in a training session.

Please subscribe to this mailing list if you would like to participate in GPF author discussions.
